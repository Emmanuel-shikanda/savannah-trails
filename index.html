Our Story.

Savannah Trails was created to share the beauty and wild spirit of EastAfrica. From the golden savannahs to incredible wildlife encounters, we design safari experiences that connect travelers with nature and adventure. We Give visitors unforgettable journeys through Africa’s most beautiful destinations.

visit Kenya.

Close your eyes and imagine warm golden sand beneath your feet, the sound of waves gently crashing, and a horizon that stretches forever. Kenya's coastline is not just a destination — it is a feeling you will never want to leave behind

visit Tanzania.

Touch the Roof of Africa "Standing at the foot of Kilimanjaro, you realize some mountains are more than just mountains. They are a calling. Rise through the clouds, conquer Africa's highest peak and come back a different person entirely."

visit Uganda.

Uganda is a stunning pearl of Africa with lush green landscapes, misty mountains, and rich wildlife. From gorilla trekking in Bwindi to the mighty Nile, it offers pure adventure and unforgettable beauty.

visit Rwanda.

Land of Fire and Mist "Rising dramatically from the earth, Rwanda's volcanic mountains are as powerful as they are beautiful. Hike through ancient lava trails wrapped in thick mist, where every turn reveals a landscape so dramatic it feels like another world entirely."

visit Burundi.

Burundi is a hidden gem of Africa, filled with rolling hills, Lake Tanganyika’s calm beauty, and rich cultural traditions that create a peaceful and authentic travel experience.

visit Ethopia.

Ethiopia is a land of ancient wonders, dramatic highlands, and deep history, where unique landscapes and historic sites like rock-hewn churches create a powerful and timeless adventure.
